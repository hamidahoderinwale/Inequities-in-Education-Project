Project 1

Analyzing Gender and Social Imbalances in the Canadian Education System

The expected outcome of the project is to help young Canadians understand the shifts in education so they can better prepare for their future and for older people in the form of faculty at post-secondary institutions, parents of young people and even corporate employers, to best understand how to support students. Education is exceptionally important however with rising tuition costs, it is important to understand whether the time and monetary investment are worth it. As a student-led investigatory data project, this was an opportunity to better understand the Canadian education landscape on a better level to better prepare for our future.

The datasets analyzed focused on undergraduate education because we felt this was the most applicable level of study that would allow for the greatest benefit amongst readers and the public who came across our research. In the future, we are hopeful that we will be able to highlight other groups of people and other components of the educational system for deeper analysis.
